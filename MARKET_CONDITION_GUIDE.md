## ğŸ“ Learning Resources

### Understanding VIX:
- VIX measures expected volatility (fear)
- Low VIX = calm market = easier to trade
- High VIX = scary market = harder to trade
- VIX spikes during crashes/uncertainty

### Understanding SPY:
- SPY tracks S&P 500 (overall market)
- When SPY trends up = bullish market
- When SPY trends down = bearish market
- When SPY sideways = ranging market

### Understanding SMAs:
- SMA20 = 20-day average price
- SMA50 = 50-day average price
- Price > SMAs = uptrend
- Price < SMAs = downtrend
